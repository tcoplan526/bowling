Please join us November 15 & 16 and November 22 & 23 for the SNHUSBC annual association tournament. We are adding $1,000 to the team prize fund! <a href="index.pdf" target="blank">Click here for a registration form and rules. </a>  

Squad times are 9am and 1pm every day. Please note the 1pm squad on November 15th is limited to 10 lanes. We suggest advanced registration if you want to bowl this squad.

Walk-ins are welcome up to 30 minutes prior to squad start times, depending on lane availability. See you there and good luck!
